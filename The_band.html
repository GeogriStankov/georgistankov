HISTORY

Rammstein is a deutsche music band playing industrial metal. The band is founded in 1994 in Berlin. Apart from its heavy sound, the band is distinguished by its remarkable presence of a great deal of theatre and drama in its music, reinforced by protruding electronic keyboard. The band is also known for its spectacular live performances. The structure of the songs is similar, consisting of heavy cut reefs, repeating during most of the duration of the songs against the backdrop of chaotic keyboards and chorus. Worldwide copies of their albums are over 25 million. The lyrics are aggressive, dark, often naturalistic or grotesque. Love themes also occur, but are most often represented by their dark side. Since its foundation the members of the band remain unchanged. Since 1994 Rammstein has released six studio albums.

MEMBERS

Vocal

Till Lindemann

Date of birth : 4th of January 1963

Place of birth : Leipzig

In addition to his vocal position in the band, he is also the main songwriter in the band. It has a strong stage presence and a deep bass voice. Lindemann appears in many soundtracks of movies such as – Triple X franchise, Resident evil, Mortal Combat, The lost highway, The Matrix and many others. Before founding the band with his colleagues, he practiced swimming and even became a European vice-champion for youngster. After completing his sporting activities, he worked as a curator of a gallery, carpenter and basket-knitter. Because of an pyrotechnical incident at a stage in Berlin, Rammstein members began to hire professional pyrotechnics, and Lindemann learned and practiced with them. Each member of the group is specifically instructed about the parts of the pyrotechnics used on stage.

Lead guitar

Richard Z. Kruspe

Date of birth : 24th of June 1967

Place of birth : Wittenberg

One of the founders of the group. As a child, he was a big fan of the rock band KISS. For him, KISS were a symbol of capitalism in its purest form. As a younger has been a wrestling champion.

Rhythm guitar

Paul Landers

Date of birth : 9th of December 1964

Place of birth : Berlin

As a child he attended violin, guitar and piano lessons.

Bass guitar

Oliver Riedel

Date of birth : 11th of April 1971

Place of birth : Schwerin

In 1994, Riedel, Lindemann, Kruspe and Schneider joined and won a musical competition in Berlin, with the opportunity to record 4 demo tracks in a professional studio. Landers and Lorenz later joined the band that they named Rammstein.

Drums

Christoph Schneider

Date of birth : 11th of May 1966

Place of birth : Berlin

At age of 16 he leaves college and starts working as a telecommunication assistant and manages to save money and buy a set of drums.

Keybords

Christian Lorenz

Date of birth : 16th of November 1966

Place of birth : Berlin

He becomes “addicted” to rock as a child. At age 16 he was apprenticed as a toy master, an obviously short career.
